Cafe with Tandoori Tea

Traditionally, cardamom, ginger, cloves, cinnamon and black peppercorns were dominant chai spices and all readily available in India. As chai moved west, bay leaf, allspice, cacao or saffron became popular additions. The clay pot imparts a smoky and earthy flavor to the tea, making it a delightful and aromatic drink. To prepare tandoori matka chai, black tea leaves are simmered with water, milk, and a blend of spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, and ginger.
